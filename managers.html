Line Manager's Role

  • 🔍 Review KRAs & KPIs — Ensure they are relevant, measurable, and aligned with school objectives
  • ✏️ Approve or Amend — Make changes with clear justification; employee will be notified
  • 📊 Score Each KPI — Rate the employee's performance against each indicator and submit
  • 📋 Review Next Year Plan — Approve the employee's 2026–27 KRAs and development plan
  • 🏁 Close the Cycle — Your submission triggers the employee's final acknowledgement and grade
